Here is the basic html template for this project:
```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
    <meta charset="UTF-8">
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
    <title>ES6 Shopping List</title>
</head>
<body>
    <div class="wrapper">
        <div class="form">
            <input type="text" name="itemToAdd" id="itemToAdd" placeholder="Add an item to your shopping list...">
            <button class="btn" id="addButton">+</button>
        </div>

        <div class="list" id="list">
            <div class="list-item">
                <input type="text" value="MILK" class="item-name" disabled />
                <div class="item-actions">
                    <button class="update">Update</button>
                    <button class="remove">Remove</button>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="list-item">
                <input type="text" value="Carrots" class="item-name" disabled />
                <div class="item-actions">
                    <button class="update">Update</button>
                    <button class="remove">Remove</button>
                </div>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
    <script src="index.js"></script>
</body>
</html>

```
